Answers to Your Top Questions

Check out our frequently asked questions to learn more about our process, services, and what to expect.
How does the reader make money?

Authors pay to have their material reviewed. When you become among the first to rank the material, you get subsidized for your effort. What you make depends on your reader credibility rank and how many votes you applied to the work you reviewed.

What is a reader's credibility rank?

A reader's credibility increases as his rating match other readers. If you rank content high while others rank them low, your credibility decreases.

How do you keep reader's joining a wandbagon to increase their credibility rating?

Readers can't see how other readers are ranking a piece of content until after the piece has been reviewed by a certain number of people.

What’s included in the subscription?

Readers subscribe to have the right to review author material. Their subscription affords them a certain number of votes which they can apply to a piece of work. Once the reader's quota is depleted, they have to wait till the next period to have their quota replenished. Or they can buy more piQues a la carte.

Can I upgrade or change my plan?

Absolutely! Avid readers may wish to increase the number of votes (we call them piQues) to apply. That way they can rate more books, or apply more of their voting power to a single piece of work.

How do I get started?

Simple! Choose a plan, subscribe, create an account and we’ll take care of the rest. You can immediately start reviewing creative content and find the next bestseller.
